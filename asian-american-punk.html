Asian American Punk

There was not much information on Asian American Punk bands. Asian Punk bands I found were mainly from Japan. I did, however, manage to find a few bands with at least one Asian American member.

Mike Park - Founder of the band Skankin' Pickle. He was also in the bands The Chinkees and The Bruce Lee Band.  He has his own record label, as well as a non-profit organization called The Plea for Peace Foundation.  Currently he is doing solo work. An interesting fact is that Mike Park is also a Minister.

Skankin' Pickle - A Ska Punk band formed in San Jose, CA and active from 1989 to 1996. Founder Mike Park went on to form his own independent record label called Asian Man Records. Skankin' Pickle covered the Bad Brains song Pay to Cum. You can hear the cover below along with two other songs - Special Brew and Violent Love.

Eskapo - Punk band from Vallejo, CA, a suburb in the Bay Area. They are Filipino and they sing in Tagaloc (a Filipino dialect) and English. On their MySpace they have a link to the San Francisco Committee for Human Rights in the Philippines.

Shoebox Confessions - Pop Punk/Alternative Rock band from Fresno, CA.
        MySpace

Beatnik Termites - Punk band formed in 1987 in Cleveland, OH. The guitarist/singer is Pat Kim, or Pat Termite. Their musical style is a mixture of Pop Punk, Oldies/doo-wop, and Surf Punk. In 1997, the covered the entire Ramones 1981 album Pleasant Dreams. Kurt Cobain from Nirvana cited the Beatnik Termites as one of his favorite bands in the Cobain Diaries.
