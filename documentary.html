Chicano Punk Documentary: Beyond the Screams



Beyond the Screams is a Chicano/Latino Punk documentary produced by Martin Sorrondeguy from the band Los Crudos.  The film is just under thirty minutes long and documents the U.S. Latino Punk movement and the DYI (do it yourself) philosophy.  Live performances from Chicano/Latino Punk bands are included, as well as a history of the movement.  The bands featured describe their music as political resistance.
